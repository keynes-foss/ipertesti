THE SCHOOL                                          


Here in London we are going to Sweandean School of English: it is a school only for foreign students, who go in England for some time.

It's a small school, but with a lot of rooms, full of people coming from everywhere. It is situated in Kingston Road, Nelsen House.

In the school, we have conversations with our teacher, Kathy, and some written exercises to improve our grammar.

We think that this kind of work is very important because it improves our English in a friendly way.

We like having conversations with Kathy because she is very open to every kind of speech, so we can talk of everything. We think that it can make ours relationships deeper.

This school is very useful, because it can improve our English that it can also make us meet new people and make new friends.

We like this school a lot.
