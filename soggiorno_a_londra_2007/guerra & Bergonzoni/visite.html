VISITS IN LONDON


Every day, after the school, we went to visit the city of London.

On the first day we saw Trafalgar Square, the National Gallery and then we passed through St. James’s Park up to Buckingham Palace. It was all wonderful!!

Then, on Monday, we went in Wimbledon to Centrecourt, a shopping centre, with our English teacher Kathy. Then, we saw St Paul’s Cathedral and the Museum of London.

On Tuesday we went to the Natural Historic Museum and to the Science Museum: two beautiful museums!!

On Wednesday we went to the Royal Observatory and the Maritime Museum, and in the evening we went to Piccadilly Circus.

On Thursday we went to the Tower of London, where we stayed all afternoon.

Then, on Friday we went to the Globe theatre , Southwark Cathedral and Clink Prison (but we didn’t like it so much!!).

On Saturday we went to the British Museum, Covent Garden, Westminster Abbey (but it was closed!!) and the House of Parliament.

Then on our last day we went to the Tower Bridge, a beautiful bridge that symbolizes London in every part of the world.

This week has been fantastic, because London is the most beautiful city in the world!


Silvia and Denise
